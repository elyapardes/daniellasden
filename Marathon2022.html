How do I register for #TEAMDANIELLA?
  1. Fill out the form below and we will email you a discount code for registration in Marathon Jerusalem 2022.
  2. Go to the Jerusalem Marathon Site and register.
  3. We will send you a link to your own personal fundraising page
  4. In our Marathon 2022 Whatsapp group, you will receive all the materials that you need to succeed at your personal campaign,
    and of course great tips for successful fundraising, and guidance and support throughout!
TOGETHER, we will succeed at establishing Beit Daniella’s first scholarship fund!
