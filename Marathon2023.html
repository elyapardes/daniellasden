Beit Daniella and Running

There’s a direct link between Beit Daniella’s vision and running in a marathon. It comes down to two things: Empowerment and Continuity!Beit Daniella strives to help teens focus on what they are capable of achieving, through willpower and consistent internal dialogue. Running a marathon is about empowering your mind and training your muscles to do things you didn’t necessarily know your body could do. Both in mental health and in marathon training one can see their ability to persevere and succeed! In Beatie Deutsch’s words: “Running in general, and specifically marathon running, taught me that there are incredible strengths that lie within each person that we might not even be aware of.”
