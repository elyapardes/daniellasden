Jerusalem Marathon 2019

What an exhilarating day it was! The combination of adrenalin, dopamine, highly oxygenated post-rain air in the beautiful city of Jerusalem and 40,000 beautiful people running..most of them to help others and support a cause, greater than themselves. DOES IT GET BETTER THAN THAT??

Our amazing Team Beit Daniella raised 113, 412 NIS and crushed it!! Superstar Team Leader Beatie Deutsch did the half marathon in 1:21:46, coming 1st place amongst Israelis and 2nd place overall. Our very own Ariella Rothschild did the full 42K! Our accountant and long time Marathon Runner Iris Stark commented, “This was my first run in Jerusalem and it was different than any run that I’ve been on.. Here people run for others and you see it in the way they run”.

Thanks to all those young and old who ran with all their heart. We are so grateful! Many thanks to our generous sponsors Healables, Meitar Law Firm Stark&Stark Accountants, Chagiga- Assaf Baloons, Kosher Casual
